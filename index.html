Leaders

Jon Tyson

Jon Tyson is an Aussie-American church planter and pastor in New York City.

He’s the author of several books and pioneer of Forming Men and Primal Path, both aimed at helping men uncover a Christian vision for their lives and for the lives of their sons.

Ronald Greene

Ronald Greene leads the Worship Arts Ministries at Bridgeway Community Church, with locations in Columbia and Owings Mills.

With the ability to cross and blend genres, and passionate about cultivating hunger for the presence of God, Ronald is a pioneering leader in multi-cultural worship.
